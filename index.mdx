## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Sign up at [hyparrow.com](https://hyparrow.com) and verify your email with the OTP sent to your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete onboarding">
    Submit your business or personal onboarding application so your account can be approved.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate an API key">
    From your dashboard, create an API key. Use it to authenticate every request to the Hyparrow API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start integrating">
    Choose a feature — bill payments, transfers, invoices, or KYC — and follow the relevant guide to go live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
